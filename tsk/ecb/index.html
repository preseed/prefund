Electrical Crepe Bandage

Scroll down

A wearable electrical crepe badge used to minimize the use of pain killer drugs. It is a step towards non-invasive, low-risk nerve stimulation intended to reduce pain, both acute and chronic. It is able to provide transcutaneous electric nerve simulation therapy along with static and pulsed magnetic field therapy


A socio-medical Movement

The device is able to produce controlled vibration with the help of special vibrator and frequency of 3hz suitable for releasing endorphin hormone. The cost of production for a single unit of crepe band will not exceed rupees 100 and even less if mass produced
